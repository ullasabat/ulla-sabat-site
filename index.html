Services

Illustration and visual storytelling for authors and publishers

☽ ✦

Book Cover Illustration

Full cover art for fantasy, dark fantasy, horror, and speculative fiction — front covers, full wraps, ebook variants, and series design systems. I work closely with authors and publishers to capture the tone and soul of your story. Commercial licensing included.

◇ ◆

Character Design & Illustration

Bring your characters from page to canvas — portrait busts, full-body illustrations, character design sheets, expression studies, and turnarounds. Perfect for author reference, promotional reveals, merchandise, and interior art.

✧ ◯

World-Building & Interior Art

Maps, sigils, magic systems, creature designs, chapter headers, scene illustrations, and concept art. Build a complete visual world for your series — from the cosmology to the costumes.

❧ ❧

Merch & Product Design

Turn your book's art into a product line — art prints, bookmarks, stickers, tarot cards, enamel pins, apparel, coloring pages, and more. Designed for conventions, Kickstarter reward tiers, and reader gifts.

✦ ✧

Branding & Social Media

Social media banners, content templates, Discord branding, moodboards, and launch campaign graphics. A cohesive visual presence for your book, your series, and your author brand.

☾ ☽

Live Illustration

Real-time character painting during author readings, convention panels, and livestreams. Entertainment meets promotion — your audience watches a character come to life while you tell their story.

Pricing varies by project scope and complexity. I'm happy to work within your budget and timeline — get in touch for a custom quote.

About

The Artist

I'm Ulla Sabat, a visual artist and storyteller. I bring modern myths to life through paint, pixels, and prose.

My work lives where art and narrative meet, weaving fantasy, folklore, and myth into compelling characters, atmospheric scenes, and immersive worlds.

When I'm not painting, I'm doing karaoke for fun, or crafting stories about sidhe courts, cursed guardians, and the complicated spaces between light and shadow.

I am a classically trained artist in both digital and traditional media and am available for commissions, book covers, character design, and collaborative projects.
